suya

African Cuisine

Nyama choma

Grilled meat, typically goat or beef, seasoned with spices and served with sides like kachumbari (a fresh tomato and onion salad).

African Cuisine

Ugali

A dense, starchy side dish made from maize flour, similar to polenta, served with stews and sauces.

African Cuisine

Injera

A spongy, sour flatbread made from teff flour, commonly used in Ethiopian and Eritrean cuisine to scoop up various dishes.

African Cuisine

Couscous

Steamed semolina grains served with meat, vegetables, and a flavorful broth.

African Cuisine

Tagine

A slow-cooked stew made in a clay pot, combining meat (often lamb or chicken) with vegetables, fruits, and aromatic spices.

African Cuisine

Harira

A rich soup made from lentils, chickpeas, and tomatoes, often eaten to break the fast during Ramadan.
